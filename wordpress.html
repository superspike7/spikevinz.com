How I approach a WordPress cleanup task

  1. Check the live issue and expected outcome.
  2. Back up the site or work on staging when available.
  3. Fix the layout, form, SEO field, page section, or plugin/theme issue.
  4. Test desktop, tablet, and mobile.
  5. Send a short summary with screenshots or notes.

How I deliver safely

Most WordPress problems are not just about changing a page. The important part is making the change safely, testing it, and leaving clear notes.

  • Review the issue and expected outcome
  • Back up or use staging when available
  • Avoid unnecessary plugin changes
  • Make the smallest safe fix first
  • Test across desktop and mobile
  • Check forms and email notifications when relevant
  • Share what changed, what was tested, and what still needs attention

What you get after each task

  • What was changed
  • What was tested
  • Any risks or follow-up notes
  • Screenshots when helpful
  • Whether it was done on staging or production
  • Recommended next steps
